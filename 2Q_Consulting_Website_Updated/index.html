About Us

At 2Q Consulting, questions drive innovation. The name "2Q" stems from founder Nate Rusch's philosophy that there are always two more questions to be asked—questions that uncover deeper insights and fuel smarter solutions.

Nate’s consulting philosophy centers on the “People, Process, Platform” ideology: understanding the people who make the organization thrive and their roles, analyzing and refining the processes by which the organization operates, and only then tailoring the technology platform to support and elevate your goals. This ensures that the solutions developed are human-centered, sustainable, and impactful.

Nate’s extensive experience in technology and consulting shapes 2Q Consulting into a trusted partner for businesses seeking growth, efficiency, and resilience. Our mission is to ask the right questions, challenge assumptions, and create custom solutions that empower organizations to thrive in an ever-changing landscape.
