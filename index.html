About Us

2Q comes from the idea that Nate always has two more questions to ask, ensuring that we explore every angle and deliver solutions tailored to your needs.

My consulting philosophy centers on the “People, Process, Platform” ideology: understanding the people who make the organization thrive and their roles, analyzing and refining the processes by which the organization operates, and only then tailoring the technology platform to support and elevate your goals. This ensures that the solutions I develop are human-centered, sustainable, and impactful.
